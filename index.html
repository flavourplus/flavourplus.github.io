Our Background

Flavour Plus is a family owned, family run business with a legacy and standard that precedes itself. Our business was established in 1988 by the late Charbel Elkazzi, growing a variety of fruits, vegetables and herbs in greenhouses. With healthy and natural growth being our focus, in both literal and economic terms, our business expanded over time assisted by our knowledge of strategic planning, business management, unique horticultural strategies, implementation of technology and automated production systems. Our achievements contributing overall to our market leading standards of health, quality, safety and efficiency.

Like any business serious about growth and longevity, the legacy was transitioned to its current, second generation director and son, Alfred Elkazzi. With Alfred at the helm, our business experiences effortless transition and adaption to new technology, staying ahead of the market and its competitors. We embrace change and continual improvement within out organisation and ensure that any new venture does not compromise our brand.

Our Product and Potential

We have always been located in North-Western Sydney, maintaining easy access to all major city centres, customers and markets. Our produce has always been grown on our 5 acre property which consists of 7500m² of fully automated greenhouses, with immediate access and plans to develop another 3500m² as part of our future projected business plan. The property has a 220m² packing shed with plenty of room and potential for future needs.

Our operations are specifically designed to provide large amounts of produce, and in the last ferw years this has mainly been continental parsley. We currently produce anywhere between 1000-1200 dozen per week between mid-October to mid-June and about 400-500 dozen per week between mid-June to mid-October. The production is currently at approximately 44000 dozen per year and can easily increase by around 30% to realise the properties full potential.

Facilities, Quality and Care

We are proudly Freshcare® certified and are fully compliant with the Freshcare® code of practice for Food Safety and Quality. This means that we guarantee extremely high agricultural practices and provide assurances that our fresh produce is safe to eat and has been prepared to meet customer requirements.

Fully Freshcare® certified supply chain solutions which ensure constant quality control from sourcing, land preparation, growing, harvesting and packing of fresh produce.

We have 24/7 remote access and automation that is used to view, maintain and update any mechanism or control of our facilities from anywhere around the world.

We grow our plants suspended 900mm off the ground in sustainably sourced "coco mix bags" which protects the parsley from any ground contaminants, as opposed to other farms who grow straight from the ground.

Sydney water is used for irrigation by drippers ensuring that all fertigation is released straight to the roots with no chance of contamination on the produce from any unwanted water or other foreign matter. The irrigation is very closely monitored for water usage to ensure maximum efficiency.

Our produce is totally enclosed in automated greenhouses giving them full protection from the external elements and environment including rain, hail, wind, vermin and animals.

Our spraying is also conducted in a ventilated and atmospheric controlled area with the use of organic sprays used largely for pest management.

We ensure the utmost care and quality control, step by step, from planting our seeds to delivering our product. Our customer service and their satisfaction is unprecedented from the very start to the very end.
